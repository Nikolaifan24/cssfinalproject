About us

At MJ Newcomer Solutions, we strive to be agents of transformation towards a more just and understanding world. Our mission is to offer comprehensive services to newcomers to Canada, not only to make their transition easier, but also to empower them as individuals so that they can contribute meaningfully to our society.
